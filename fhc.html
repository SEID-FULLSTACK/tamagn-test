How it works

የመመሪያ ክፍል

Three clear steps before you apply for FHC housing or rental programmes.

  1. 1

    Review eligibility

    Confirm income bands, household size, and programme rules in the official FHC circular for the tender you target.

  2. 2

    Prepare documents

    Gather ID, tax ID, proof of income, and completed application forms from the downloads below.

  3. 3

    Submit through official channels

    File online or at designated FHC offices before the published closing date. Keep your receipt or reference number.
